How to play Tug of War


How to Set Up


  1. Mark the center of the ground with a cone
  2. Place tape on the rope to mark the center of the rope
  3. Determine who the “Judge” will be
  4. Lay rope on the ground aligning both centers
  5. Place gloves on for more grip (optional)

(example of what the rope should look like)

How to Play


  1. Split up into two even teams
  2. Each team member must grab the rope
  3. Once the “Judge” says to begin start pulling the rope towards you
  4. Keep pulling until the center of the rope passes the center cone
  5. Then we have winner

(example of how the game should look once it has begun)
